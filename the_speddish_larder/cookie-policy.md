# Cookie Policy - The Speddish Larder

**Last Updated:** November 11, 2025

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website or use a mobile application. They are widely used to make applications work more efficiently and to provide information to application owners.

## Our Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Speddish Larder mobile application uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

### 1. Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the application to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.

- **Authentication**: To keep you logged in and recognize your account
- **Security**: To maintain application security and prevent fraud
- **Preferences**: To remember your settings and preferences (language, theme, etc.)
- **Functionality**: To enable core application features like shopping list sharing and recipe sync

### 2. Performance and Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our application.

- **Firebase Analytics**: To collect anonymous usage data and improve app performance
- **Crash Reporting**: To identify and fix technical issues
- **Performance Monitoring**: To measure app speed and responsiveness

### 3. Advertising Cookies
These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests.

- **AdMob**: To display personalized advertisements within the app
- **Google Ads**: To track ad performance and user engagement
- **Personalization**: To tailor ads based on your usage patterns

### 4. Third-Party Cookies
Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our application.

- **Google Services**: For authentication, analytics, and advertising
- **Firebase**: For data storage and backend services

## Cookie Categories Used

| Category | Purpose | Duration | Controller |
|----------|---------|----------|------------|
| **Essential** | App functionality, authentication, security | Session to 1 year | The Speddish Larder |
| **Analytics** | Usage statistics, performance monitoring | 2 years | Google (Firebase) |
| **Advertising** | Personalized ads, marketing analytics | 90 days to 1 year | Google (AdMob) |
| **Functional** | User preferences, settings storage | 1 year | The Speddish Larder |

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Mobile Application Settings
Within The Speddish Larder app, you can manage your preferences through:

1. **Settings Menu**: Access privacy settings from the app's main settings
2. **Advertising Preferences**: Opt in/out of personalized advertising
3. **Analytics**: Choose whether to contribute to anonymous usage statistics

### Device-Level Controls
You can also manage cookies at the device level:

- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID & Opt out of ads personalization
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

### Browser Cookies
If you access any of our web services through a browser, you can manage cookies through:
- Browser settings to block or delete cookies
- Privacy extensions and add-ons
- Incognito/private browsing modes

## Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:

- **Consent**: Choose whether to allow non-essential cookies
- **Information**: Know what cookies are being used and why
- **Access**: Request information about cookies stored on your device
- **Object**: Opt out of certain types of cookies, particularly advertising
- **Withdraw**: Change your cookie preferences at any time

## Cookie Duration

The retention periods for cookies used in our app:

- **Session Cookies**: Deleted when you close the app
- **Persistent Cookies**: Remain on your device for the period specified in the table above
- **Authentication Tokens**: Up to 30 days of inactivity, then require re-login
- **Analytics Cookies**: Typically 2 years
- **Advertising Cookies**: 90 days to 1 year

## International Data Transfers

Some cookies may transfer data to countries outside your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

- **EU-US Data Privacy Framework**: For transfers between EU and US
- **Standard Contractual Clauses**: For other international transfers
- **Google Cloud Infrastructure**: Complies with international data protection standards
